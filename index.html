About

Echolux is a minimal iOS camera app that transforms digital photography into authentic analog film experiences. Capture photos with real-time film emulation filters, delivering the look and feel of vintage film with modern digital precision.

Key Features

Real-time film simulation filters
Dynamic lens detection for iPhone cameras
Professional watermarking with lens and filter info
Mirror-corrected front camera
High-resolution HEIF capture
Consistent orientation handling
